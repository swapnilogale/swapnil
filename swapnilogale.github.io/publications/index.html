Talk - Making Content Sustainable

In this talk, I offer my recommendations on making our content sustainable, especially now when our content is growing at an unprecedented rate. As part of the conference theme, I compare content with the key principles of sustainability such as reducing waste, creating maximum impact, placing emphasis on longevity and relevance, and providing a sense of social responsibility or governance.
